What Goes on in the Anime Club?

The basic schedule for a club meeting goes as follows:

  • 6:00-7:00| Social Hour: Its where you socialize with other club members, you can bring games etc.
  • 7:00-7:15| Announcements: When the officers announce upcoming events in the club and other goings-on
  • 7:30-9:30| Anime Watching: We watch the chosen anime for the semester

Each semester we vote on two anime to watch. One 24 episode anime and one 12 episode anime. Then, over the course of the semester, we watch the anime each meeting, 2 episodes of the 24 and one episode of the 12. This semester we are watching "Mob Psycho 100" and "The Devil is a Part-Timer"
